Showing Einstein's famous formula using MathML

E = m c 2

Despite being in the HTML specification since a long time, developers could not make use of it until recently. Explain why that might be the case:

The issue with MathML for a long period of time was that it wasn't supported by major browsers such as Chrome or Edge. They both first introduced MathML to their browsers at the end of 2022 so developers couldn't really make use of it before when two of the most popular browsers didn't adapt to this feature. But by now over 90% of all users can access this feature over their browser, so this is not an issue anymore.
